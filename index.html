    About Us

    Agrifoodhub is a social enterprise that aims to continuously improve the agricultural sector by matching buyer requirements with farmer harvest and production information. Since 2020, Agrifoodhub has onboarded thousands of buyers and farmer groups through channels including our website, social media pages, and hotlines. It is our goal to develop the capability of our farmers to serve the ever-growing demands of the market.

    The support we offer include buyer-farmer linkage, farmer capability development, accreditations assistance, communications and logistics support, and quality assurance guidance to name a few. We are a team of professionals dedicated to improving the quality of life of our farmers and supporting the long-term growth of our partner buyers.

    FAQ

    What is the AgriFoodHub?

    Agrifoodhub is a not-for-profit organization who helps our local farmers particularly in the North and south luzon area.

    What can I do with AgriFoodHub?

    You can buy and sell your agri products and get access to services such as financing technology, storage and transportation.

    How to register in Agrifoodhub?

    Just click the “Registration” button.

    How can we help our local farmers?

    We can help our local farmers by buying directly to them.

    How can I be part of AFH as a buyer and as a Seller?

    Just fill up the registration form and our buyer and seller team will contact you.

    How might I get in touch with you?

    You can visit our Facebook page and other social media accounts
