    About Us

    AgriFood Hub is a social enterprise project that started with a question: Why are the farmers who toil under the sun, battered by storms and flood, tread knee deep mud earn so little to produce food that feeds us while many, who work in air conditioned offices get comfortable incomes?

    Seeking the answer brought us to confront a fragmented, opaque, multi-level supply chain that needed some fixing.

    We, the founders of AgriFood Hub, believe that linking farmers by a digital platform to a predictable, diverse market can help.

    We as a team have volunteered our time to package a bunch of solutions in funding, logistics and science learning to further improve the farmers.We hope to slowly but persistently elevate farm income and productivity by driving the timely sharing of information.

    FAQ

    What is the AgriFoodHub App?

    AgriFoodHub app is where farmers and buyers can meet. Farmers can post their fresh produce and Buyers and post the requirements.

    What can I do with AgriFoodHub?

    You can buy and sell your agri products, get access to services such and financing, technology, storage and transportation.

    How do I register to use AgriFoodHub?

    It's simple! Just download the app and create a profile during the log-in screen.

    Do I need to pay for using AgriFoodHub?

    AgriFoodHub is absolutely free to use.

    Can I use AgriFoodHub even without internet connection?

    You will need to be connected to the internet to use the AFH app.

    Which version of Android will the AgriFoodHub app run?

    We made sure that farmers, most especially, can use the AgriFoodHub app. So AgriFoodHub will function from version 4.4 up to the latest Android version.
